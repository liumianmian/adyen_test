Fraud score distribution of transactions routed through RevenueProtect

Each risk check is connected to a fraud score value. The total fraud score of a transaction is the sum of all the risk checks that were triggered. This chart is a histogram and shows the distribution of the fraud scores from all transactions routed through Adyen's RevenueProtect (in the chosen period). Each bar is a grouping of all transactions that had a fraud score in between the range indicated by the tooltip. The height of each bar indicates the number of transactions in the corresponding fraud score range.
