Why is the authorisation rate over 100%?

The authorisation rate is calculated as the number of authorised transactions over the number of received transactions in a given day. Although for most payment methods, transactions are authorised (or refused) shortly after being received, some payment methods can have a long delay. As a result of this, it's possible that the number of authorisations may be greater than the number of received payments on certain days.

The numbers used here are consistent with those in our financial reporting.
